About Custom Golf Cart Solutions

We don't sell carts off a shelf. We engineer the right vehicle for your specific needs — and we stand behind every build.

Who We Are

Purpose-Built. Not Off the Shelf.

Custom Golf Cart Solutions was born from a simple observation: most golf carts on the market are generic. One size, one colour, one purpose. But the customers who come to us — golf clubs, estates, hotels, warehouses, event operators — they need something built around their world, not retrofitted into it.

As a sister company to Custom Plant Solutions, we draw on decades of hands-on engineering and manufacturing expertise. That foundation means we can go further than a typical cart dealer — configuring seating, performance, branding, cargo setups, and specialist accessories to exactly match what our customers need on the ground.

  • 2-seater and 2+2 seater builds — electric and petrol
  • Custom seating, canopy, branding and colour configurations
  • Utility, cargo and fleet modifications
  • 2-year warranty with full after-sales and spare parts support
  • Nationwide delivery across South Africa

Powered by Experience

Our roots in Custom Plant Solutions give us something most cart dealers simply don't have: a deep engineering backbone. We approach every build with the same rigour as a custom plant project — thinking through purpose, load, terrain, branding, and lifecycle before a single bolt is tightened.

Configured for Purpose

Quality That Lasts

A Team That Shows Up
