FAQ

Common Questions

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes, we deliver to all major cities and towns across South Africa. Delivery costs vary by location.

How long does a custom build take?

Standard custom builds take 4—6 weeks from order confirmation. Complex builds may take up to 8 weeks.

What warranty do your carts come with?

All our carts come with a standard 2-year warranty. Extended 3-year warranties are available on custom builds.

Do you offer trade-ins?

Yes! We accept trade-ins on select models. Contact us with your cart details for a valuation.

Can you brand carts with our club logo?

Absolutely. We offer full wrap printing and logo application for clubs, resorts, and estates.

Is finance available?

Yes, we offer flexible finance and leasing options. Ask our team for a personalised finance quote.
