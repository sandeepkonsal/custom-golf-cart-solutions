Who We Are

15 Years of Driving Excellence

Founded in 2010, Custom Golf Cart Solutions was born out of a simple passion — a love for golf and a drive to build something better. What started as a small workshop in Johannesburg has grown into South Africa's most trusted name in custom golf carts.

We serve golf clubs, private estates, game lodges, resorts, and individual buyers across the country. Every cart we build or supply is held to the highest standard of quality, safety, and craftsmanship — because we know that your cart represents your brand, your course, and your pride.

  • Over 500 custom carts delivered nationwide
  • Partnerships with 50+ golf clubs & estates
  • Certified technicians with 15+ years experience
  • Full after-sales service & spare parts availability
  • Eco-friendly electric cart range available
