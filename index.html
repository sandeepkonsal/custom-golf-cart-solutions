The Custom Golf Cart Difference

We don't just sell golf carts. We craft personalised solutions built around your terrain, style, and budget.

Premium Quality

Every cart is built with top-grade components from trusted global suppliers, ensuring durability and peak performance on any terrain.

Fully Customisable

Choose your colours, seating, canopy style, accessories, and drivetrain. We build the cart that matches your vision exactly.

Electric and Petrol

We stock and build both electric and petrol-powered carts. Go eco-friendly with silent electric models or choose petrol for tougher terrain.

Expert Team

Our technicians have over 15 years of experience in golf cart sales, customisation, and servicing across South Africa.

Dedicated Support

We are with you long after the sale. Our after-sales team provides maintenance, repairs, and spare parts to keep your cart running perfectly.

Flexible Finance

We offer competitive finance packages to make owning your golf cart more accessible, for individuals, estates, and golf clubs alike.
